Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dealstrike ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and profile photo (if you sign in with Google or Apple), collected via Firebase Authentication.
  • Usage data: app interactions, screen views, and general usage patterns, collected via Google Analytics for Firebase.
  • Device information: device type, operating system, and a push notification token used to deliver deal alerts.
  • User content: items you save to your shopping list and your notification preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To send you push notifications about deals and price drops you've opted into
  • To sync your saved items and preferences across sessions
  • To understand app usage and improve features and performance
  • To communicate with you about your account, including email verification

3. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following third-party services to operate:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, database storage, and push notifications
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: anonymized usage and install analytics
  • Google Sign-In / Apple Sign-In: optional social login

These providers may process your data in accordance with their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase's cloud infrastructure. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • You may update or delete your account at any time from within the App.
  • You may disable push notifications from your device settings or in-app preferences.
  • You may request a copy or deletion of your personal data by contacting us below.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
